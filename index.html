WHAT IS COBA ?

A cup of coffee equals a COBA.

COBA is made from bean to bar, while coffee as a drink only utilizes 20% of the bean.

No more spills. No more coffee runs. COBA whenever, wherever.

Our Project

WE HAVE TWO OPTIONS

Dark COBA

Dark COBA is VEGAN with NO milk powder. It has a stronger coffee profile with the same amount of caffeineas a cup of coffee.

Milk COBA

Milk COBA is our original bar, containing milk powder to provide a more balanced and neutral taste profile for our backers.
