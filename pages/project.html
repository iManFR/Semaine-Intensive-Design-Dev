Discover COBA Coffee Bar, the solution to your coffee needs. It's very simple. We have transformed the classic coffee into a bar. The result is more efficiency and much more practical use.

We are very attentive to the quality of our products. To do this, we negotiate directly with Peruvian producers. Our coffee beans are therefore from fair trade.

Once the seeds have arrived from Peru, we carry out the transformation in our small workshop. We make our own products.

We offer 2 types of Coffee bars. One with milk and one "nature". Thus we want to satisfy all types of customers.

The Kickstarter will allow us to improve our workshop to increase our production and - perhaps - sell one day all over the world.
